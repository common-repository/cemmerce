== Description ==

Cemmerce automatically converts your product references into live commerce links. In addition, we provide access to precise and real time price comparison data, dramatically reducing abandonment rate, increasing conversion rates and generating more revenue for you!

You won't need to connect with multiple affiliate programs and manage them. You won't need to search for specific retailer's product links to update your post. Our platform will scan, and find product references anywhere in your content, monetizing even historical content.

You can find more informatio on: www.cemmerce.com

== Installation ==

1. Search in WordPress Plugin library for "Cemmerce" and click "Install now" 
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Copy your Publisher ID from your Cemmerce Account, and update it under "Cemmerce Setting" TAB (Left menu)
